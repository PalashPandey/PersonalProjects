Our mission is to help our nonprofit clients do a better job

<tr>
<td class="default"><span class="smallertext">Job Description: 

The co-op students will be essential members of our Data 
Visualization Group (DVG). They will help YPTC develop its 
data visualization skills and, by extension, they will have 
meaningful impact on the performance of our nonprofit 
clients and the lives of their constituents. 
    
The successful co-op student will be a creative person with 
an entrepreneurial spirit who possesses the ability to think 
independently “outside the box.” This intern will work with 
one to three other interns in a cross-disciplinary team, with 
interns coming from a variety of majors and backgrounds. 
Though YPTC is an accounting firm, our clients are not 
accountants, so interns with varied backgrounds that 
mirror our clients are attractive to us. 
    
This position will be based in our Center-City Philadelphia 
office, will report to the Data Visualization Group Manager 
and will work closely with the President of our company. 

Co-ops will work a full-time schedule of 35 hours per week, 
9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday, with an hour 
off for lunch. Co-ops will be paid for any YPTC paid holidays 
that occur during their internship. Paid Interns will also 
receive a proportional share of paid vacation, sick, and 
personal time, plus any paid holidays that fall 
during their co-op period.
    
YPTC will supply the technology it considers necessary to 
enable the interns to succeed.
    
Co-ops will be expected to sign a non-
disclosure/confidentiality agreement. 
    
Duties and Responsibilities:

(1) Review data visualization tools and techniques 
currently being used by YPTC staff in their reporting to 
clients. 

(2) Propose superior ways of visually displaying key 
financial and non-financial information needed by our 
clients. Examples include dashboards, video, and 
interactive displays on computers, tablets, and smart 
phones.

(3) Research, test, and evaluate data visualization tools 
and techniques currently available for disseminating 
financial and non-financial information. Such tools will 
include: Excel, accounting software, and apps available for 
smart phones and tablet computers. Both cloud based and 
locally installed solutions will be considered. 

(4) Prepare recommendations to YPTC management of 
those tools and techniques that appear most promising for 
use by our staff when they report to their clients. 

(5) Assist with the implementation of the recommended 
data visualization tools and techniques by helping to train 
YPTC staff in their use and by helping to troubleshoot 
problems during implementation. 
    
Learning objectives: 
Co-ops will have opportunities to develop team working 
skills by interacting and collaborating with other interns, 
with senior YPTC management, and with YPTC’s 
professional staff. Interns will have the opportunity to learn 
skills required for successfully developing 
recommendations of their preferred data visualization tools 
and techniques, and they will learn how to present their 
recommendations in a way as to encourage others to 
accept them. There will be opportunities to develop 
business writing skills as they draft recommendations, 
business memorandum, and training program materials. 
They will have opportunities to learn and improve their 
public speaking skills when they present their 
recommendations to our staff. Interns will also have 
opportunities to develop and conduct training classes for 
our staff. 

CO-OP STUDENTS WILL HAVE THE OPPORTUNITY TO 
INTERACT DIRECTLY WITH OUR CLIENTS FOR THE 
PURPOSE OF BETTER UNDERSTANDING THEIR DATA 
VISUALIZATION NEEDS.

In summary: this is an excellent learning opportunity for 
the right independent thinking, entrepreneurial minded, 
individual.
